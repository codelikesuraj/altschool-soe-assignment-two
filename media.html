Surpassing my limit

Applying to AltSchool Africa to pick up a new skill seems pretty ballsy if you ask me.
I mean, as a Backend Developer you'd expect I'd want to improve on that or pick up Frontend.
But no, here I am diving head first into Cloud Engineering. Let's just say I'm following my intuition.
For a while now, I've found myself having to log into servers to perform routine google search powered tasks :)
So I guess deciding to formally learn this doesn't seem counter-intuitive at all.
Learning something new can be frustrating at first. No one ever said growth was simple.
So each time I feel frustrated, I remember my favorite character, Ippo, from the anime, Hajime No Ippo, saying,
"I'm prepared to go past my limit!"
